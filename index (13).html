Why This Progression Works

The Textiles Synergy: Nearly every capstone ability — Loose Ends, Cross-Stitch, The Full Bolt — is a Thread-dump, not a raw damage scaler. A Weaver who never bothers to apply their binds simply won't have the Thread to make their big moments hit hard. The class rewards constant, active control over passive stat-stacking.

The Bind-or-Bust Gate: Web Sense, Reinforced Weave, and Silk Armor all key off currently active binds, not the Weaver's own stats. A Weaver who lets their tethers lapse loses their dodge bonus, their allies lose their damage reduction, and their shields stop generating.

The Fantasy Arc: Level 1 — a garment worker with a sidearm sewing kit, throwing bobbins and hoping the thread holds. Level 50 — a battlefield controller wired with mono-filament, binding entire fronts of enemies together. Level 99 — something closer to fate itself, healer and executioner running through the exact same thread, at the exact same moment.
